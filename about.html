About BlockchainsSpecialist

A full-service Web3 company helping founders launch coins, tokens, dApps, and exchange listings since 2014.

We Don't Just Code — We Build Businesses

BlockchainsSpecialist started with a simple mission: help entrepreneurs navigate the complex world of blockchain without getting burned by bad advice or failed launches.

Over 8+ years, we've deployed 75+ coins and tokens, secured listings on KuCoin, Gate.io, Coinsbit, Dex-Trade, and more, and helped clients raise funding through successful ICO campaigns.

Today we're a full company — not a freelancer portfolio. We offer consultation, development, listing support, and ongoing partnership for projects at every stage.
